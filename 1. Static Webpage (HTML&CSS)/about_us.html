About Us



General


Greatest Wars Site aims to provide quick, convenient, and reliable access to the greatest wars in history. It's perfect for people who don't have enough time to read a whole detailed article. Only the most important information of each war appears on this site.


The creator


The "Greatest War Site" was created in 2019 by Olympia Axelou, student at the University of Thessaly, Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering. This site was created as an assignment for the Web Development course. I was asked to create a simple, static website with HTML and CSS.In order to accomplish this, I watched a series of HTML tutorials and CSS tutorials. The HTML course I followed is here and the CSS course is here.
